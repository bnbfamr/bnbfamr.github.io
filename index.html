Structured RWA Protocol

Famr RWA integrates motion poultry-backed surreal world assets with tiered NFT emission and a deflationary Add-Burn liquidity model.


• Tokens are not added to the StakePool until after the initial 200 NFT mints and over 89.9% BNB is received.

• Only miners have the authority to increase or mint FAMR assets into circulation.

• 800 Trillion FAMR (80% of total supply) is permanently burned after 200 NFT mints.
