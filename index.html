FAMR NFT Architecture

The FAMR Poultry Collection is deployed under the ERC-1155 standard, allowing semi-fungible tier classification. Each NFT class represents a staking power bracket with predefined APY and burn conversion weight.

Add-Burn Liquidity Model

Post-initial mint cycles utilize collected BNB to purchase FAMR from the liquidity pool and permanently burn tokens, reinforcing price floor and reducing circulating supply.
